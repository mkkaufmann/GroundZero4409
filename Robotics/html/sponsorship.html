Sponsor Us

Help keep our team running.

Our team is always open to anyone that wants to support us. You can help in many ways, including:

  • Mentoring our team in an area you are familiar with
  • Supporting us at the lab or at competition
  • Aiding us financially

It would be impossible to run our team without sponsors. If you are able to support us in any of these ways, it would mean a lot to us. If you want to come visit our lab to check us out, you are welcome to. Sponsors always have their names on our robot, and often have us tour their facility or frequently visit ours.
Here is a list of our current sponsors:

  • AEP
  • Chassix
  • Upton Foundation
  • Indiana Blacksmithing Association
  • Others (omitted until the full list is acquired)

You could be on this list along with all of these companies and people with a donation of any type or amount. We appreciate your support more than you could imagine.
