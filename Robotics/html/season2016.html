2016 Season

Back when we won a competition.

During the 2016 Season, our team was much smaller. Many team members agree that the yearly 'games' became much better starting in 2016. The 2016 game involved your robot driving over obstacles to break their opponent's 'defenses' for points. Robots could also shoot or roll 'boulders' at the 'castles' and then climb up to a bar at the end for even more points.

Our team did pretty well during the season. The robot could do everything, but it couldn't do anything particularly well. Most of the people that experienced the 2016 season would agree that the robot was pretty 'janky.'

The team tried their best, but only came in one of the last places at the St. Joseph Event. However, at the East Kentwood competition, the team were part of the winning alliance, along with the Comets (3357) and Code Red Robotics (2771). Sadly, even after winning East Kentwood, we did not qualify for Michigan State Finals.
